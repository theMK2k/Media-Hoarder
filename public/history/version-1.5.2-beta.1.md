# Media Hoarder v1.5.2-beta.1 Changelog

Fixes and changes of v1.5.2-beta.1:

### Features

- [x] support of zoom levels
  - range: 50% - 300%
  - adjustable in Settings | General or everywhere with `CTRL` + `+` / `-` or `CTRL` + mouse wheel `up` / `down`
  - zoom levels are stored and survive restarts
