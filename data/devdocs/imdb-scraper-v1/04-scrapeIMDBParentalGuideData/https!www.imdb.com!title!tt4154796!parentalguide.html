    Frightening & Intense Scenes

    • Of all the MCU films, this one is easily the darkest and most depressing film in the franchise. It's much more emotional and intense than "Infinity War", and despite its fair share of comedic moments, the entire film has the tone, look and feel of a dystopian movie, akin to "Logan (2017)". There's a persistent sense of hopelessness throughout the film, and all of this might be too much for some viewers who are familiar with the previous MCU films.

    Violence & Gore

    • A masked man fights numerous people on a street and in buildings (people are stabbed and gunshots are heard). He then fights a man, slicing his throat. Although the action is not directly seen, blood can be seen trickling down the man's neck onto his hand.

    • In a moment that mirrors Gamora's death in "Infinity War", Black Widow sacrifices herself to make Hawkeye earn the Soul Stone by leaping off a cliff. We then see her corpse lying on the ground, with a pool of blood around her.
      Edit
    • During the battle at the Avengers' headquarters, the final battle between the Avengers and their now restored allies against Thanos, numerous filler characters/minions die, including getting blown up, tossed about, stepped on, impaled, blasted or shot, etc. None of it is bloody or dwelt on, less so than the climactic battle of Wakanda in "Infinity War", but it's still rather brutal and it has an even higher body count.
      Edit
    • Tony Stark is mortally wounded after snapping his fingers in an intense confrontation with Thanos. His entire body has severe burns, and it's all shown in gruesome detail, complete with several sores and blisters all over his body and later dies from this, as his grieving wife, Pepper Potts, and his friends mourn his death. This scene is very emotional and disturbing.

    Frightening & Intense Scenes

    • Many people are killed during the intense third act war scene.
      Edit
    • A scene where a ship opens fire on a building and we see characters inside the building struggle is quite intense.
      Edit
    • Hawkeye and Black Widow fight each other on the edge of a cliff so the other person doesn't have to be the sacrifice for the soul stone. Eventually, Black Widow decides to sacrifice herself, resulting in her tragic death. This is an extremely emotional and intense scene, and adding to the fact that Black Widow is a much beloved character by the fans, only makes it even worse.
      Edit
    • The death(s) of major character(s) can be very emotional.
      Edit
    • In one particular scene, some monsters chase a character down a dark tunnel. It can be frightening for younger viewers.
      Edit
    • 2 main characters die, and the movie can be very emotional for viewers
      Edit
    • In a scene, Nebula's prosthetic hand melts when she picks up the Power Stone, revealing a skeletal structure. This may be frightening for some.
      Edit
    • Thor is seen suffering from severe PTSD in this film, due to the traumatic events he had throughout, especially after losing against Thanos. Because of this, he's shown drinking heavily to relieve himself, to the point of becoming overweight and gaining a bulging beer belly, and he also panics when reminded of this, as shown by the fact that he instantly freaks out when Hulk mention Thanos in front of him. This can be disturbing for some people.
      Edit
    • The last 10 minutes (after the climactic battle) are very emotional, as the Avengers and their allies end up parting ways, which may affect those who where familiar with their relationships as a team on a emotional level.
      Edit
    • Tony Stark's death following his sacrifice to defeat Thanos can (and should) be especially uncomfortable to anyone watching the film. What makes it even more upsetting and disturbing is that he's horribly burned and shares his last moments surrounded by his grieving friends before finally dying in his wife Potts' arms, who breaks down into tears after he dies. Add to Tony Stark's status as a fan favorite, and this is an incredibly shocking, graphic, upsetting, and heart-wrenching scene.
